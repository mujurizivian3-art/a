💛 About Agnes & Family Hotel

Established in 2005, we are a family-run hotel known for warmth and authentic flavors.

Our Story

Agnes & Family Hotel started as a small guesthouse with a big heart. Over the years, we’ve grown into a beloved hospitality spot, blending modern comfort with traditional African hospitality. Every guest is treated like family, and our kitchen serves recipes passed down through generations.

We believe in sustainable tourism, supporting local farmers, and creating memorable experiences — whether you’re staying overnight or just joining us for a meal.

⭐ Rated 4.8/5 by hundreds of happy guests. We invite you to become part of our story.

Our motto: "Your happiness, our pride."
